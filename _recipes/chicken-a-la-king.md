title: Chicken à la King
short_name: Chicken à la King
tagline: "The ultimate comfort food."
source: "Adapted from The Cozy Cook"
serves: 6
prep_time: "15 mins"
cook_time: "45 mins"
main_ingredients: [chicken breast, mushrooms, cream, peas, white wine]
star_ingredient: poultry
tags: [freezable, "make-ahead", "one-handed food"]
ingredient_groups:
  - name: "sauce"
    items:
      - "1 tbsp olive oil"
      - "600 g boneless skinless chicken breast"
      - "1 large onion, diced"
      - "2 medium carrots, diced"
      - "2 stalks celery, diced"
      - "3 cloves of garlic, minced"
      - "125 ml dry white wine"
      - "40 g flour"
      - "250 ml chicken stock"
      - "Flavourings like onion powder, English mustard powder, Worcestershire sauce, mushroom ketchup, cayenne pepper to taste."
      - "250 g mushrooms"
      - "125 g peas"
      - "Herbs to taste, preferably fresh"
      - "200 ml creme fraiche or single cream"
method:
  - "Pat the chicken dry and season. Fry for 3 mins each side. Remove from heat and rest for 10 minutes."
  - "Sweat the onions, carrots and celery until soft and golden, then increase the heat to brown them. Add the minced garlic 2 mins before you're done."
  - "Deglaze with  a splash of white wine, scraping all the bits off the bottom of the pan, then reduce the heat to medium. Sprinkle in the flour and stir to combine."
  - "Chop the chicken and add it back to the pan. Add the rest of the wine and simmer for a minute or two. Then add the stock, and bring to the boil while stirring continuously. After it boils, you can stop stirring. Let it cook for about 5 mins. Season and flavour to taste." 
  - "Add the mushrooms then boil for 5 mins until softened."
  - "Add the peas and any fresh herbs, and simmer uncovered for 5–10 more minutes until thickened. Check seasoning."
  - "Stir through the creme fraiche or cream at the end, and cook for 1 more minute."
method_short:
  - |
    * Sear chicken 3 mins per side
       * Prep onions/carrots/celery
    * Fry, garlic, deglaze with white wine, flour, chicken, stock, boil 5 mins, mushrooms 5 mins
    * Simmer 10 mins
    * Peas/whatever, creme fraiche 
notes:
  - "You can chuck any vegetables in here and it'll still be comforting."
  - "ANNOTATION Don't panic if the flour goes clumpy, all will be well, just don't stop stirring until it boils."
  - "ANNOTATION Button mushrooms are always fun to eat in one-handed food, especially if you don't halve them, because then you get to chase them around your plate."

main_ingredients: [chicken thighs and drumsticks, cider, carrots, cooking apples, crème fraîche]
star_ingredient: poultry
tags: ["one-handed food", "one-pot"]
ingredient_groups:
  - items:
      - "1.5 kg chicken thighs and drumsticks"
      - "2 large onions, diced"
      - "4 medium carrots, diced, and extra in chunks if you like them"
      - "4 stalks celery, diced"
      - "4 cloves garlic, minced"
      - "2 tbsp olive oil"
      - "350 ml medium-dry cider"
      - "3 tarragon sprigs"
      - "200 g summer turnips, peeled and chopped"
      - "2 large cooking apples, chopped"
      - "300 g green beans"
      - "150 g crème fraîche"
      - "handful fresh chives and/or parsley"
      - "seasoning and flavourings -- see note"
method:
  - "Toss the chicken in olive oil then season."
  - "Fry in batches, 2 mins per side, turning once, until there's a golden brown crust. Remove from pan to rest."
  - "Sweat onions, carrots and celery until soft and golden, then increase the heat to brown them. Add the garlic when you're 2 mins from done."
  - "Return the chicken to the pan."
  - "Add cider to deglaze, then the tarragon, and bring to the boil. Then simmer gently for 40 mins."
  - "Add more carrots if you like, and turnips and apples, then simmer for 10–15 mins."
  - "Add the beans, increase the heat, and cook for 5 mins."
  - "Add the crème fraîche, stir to combine, and heat for another 2 mins."
  - "Add the parsley and/or chives to serve."
method_short:
  - |
    * Sear chicken
       * Prep onion/carrots/celery
    * Fry, deglaze with cider, season, herbs, simmer 40 mins
    * Carrots, turnips, apples, simmer 10-15 mins
    * Beans boil 5 mins
    * Creme fraiche 2 mins
notes:
  - "Season and flavour the liquid with stock cubes, mushroom ketchup, anchovy sauce, Worcestershire sauce, English mustard powder, chicken stock cubes, bay leaves, lemon juice, olive oil, anything: whatever it needs and whatever you like."
